Test Case: Searching for a Product and Adding it to the Cart
Observation: It's not necessary is logged in the site. You can add items in the cart. 


Step 1: Open the browser and navigate to https://www.advantageonlineshopping.com
Expected Result: The homepage is displayed.
Search for a product

Step 2: Enter "Laptop" in the search box and click the search button.
Expected Result: Search results for "Laptop" are displayed.
Select the first product

Step 3: Click on the first product in the search results.
Expected Result: Product detail page of the selected product is displayed.
Add the product to the cart

Step4 : Click on the "Add to Cart" button.
Expected Result: The product is added to the cart, and a confirmation message is displayed.
